### Types of Deployments

- All at once

      - Deploy all the instances at the same time
      - Fastest deployment
      - Downtime

- Rolling

      - Deploy instances in batches
      - No downtime
      - Slowest deployment

- Blue/Green
    
        - Deploy new instances alongside the old ones
        - No downtime
        - Fast deployment
